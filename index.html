The Perfect PB & J

Description

This peanut butter jelly sandwich is my favorite sandwich. It has the perfect balance of ingridents and looks great when made right.

Any peanut butter and jam will work but in my recommendation is to use grape jelly and all natural peanut butter.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of natural peanut butter

Directions

  1. Get a butter knife.
  2. Open the peanut butter and jelly.
  3. Spread both ingredients onto the pieces of bread.
  4. Eat.
